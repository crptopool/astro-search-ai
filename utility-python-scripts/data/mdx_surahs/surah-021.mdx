            <Aside type="quran" title="1">
            ٱقْتَرَبَ لِلنَّاسِ حِسَابُهُمْ وَهُمْ فِى غَفْلَةٍۢ مُّعْرِضُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="1">
            ˹The time of˺ people’s judgment has drawn near, yet they are heedlessly turning away.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="2">
            مَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن ذِكْرٍۢ مِّن رَّبِّهِم مُّحْدَثٍ إِلَّا ٱسْتَمَعُوهُ وَهُمْ يَلْعَبُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="2">
            Whatever new reminder comes to them from their Lord, they only listen to it jokingly,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="3">
            لَاهِيَةًۭ قُلُوبُهُمْ ۗ وَأَسَرُّوا۟ ٱلنَّجْوَى ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ هَلْ هَٰذَآ إِلَّا بَشَرٌۭ مِّثْلُكُمْ ۖ أَفَتَأْتُونَ ٱلسِّحْرَ وَأَنتُمْ تُبْصِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="3">
            with their hearts ˹totally˺ distracted. The evildoers would converse secretly, ˹saying,˺ “Is this ˹one˺ not human like yourselves? Would you fall for ˹this˺ witchcraft, even though you can ˹clearly˺ see?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="4">
            قَالَ رَبِّى يَعْلَمُ ٱلْقَوْلَ فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="4">
            The Prophet responded, “My Lord ˹fully˺ knows every word spoken in the heavens and the earth. For He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="5">
            بَلْ قَالُوٓا۟ أَضْغَٰثُ أَحْلَٰمٍۭ بَلِ ٱفْتَرَىٰهُ بَلْ هُوَ شَاعِرٌۭ فَلْيَأْتِنَا بِـَٔايَةٍۢ كَمَآ أُرْسِلَ ٱلْأَوَّلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="5">
            Yet they say, “This ˹Quran˺ is a set of confused dreams! No, he has fabricated it! No, he must be a poet! So let him bring us a ˹tangible˺ sign like those ˹prophets˺ sent before.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="6">
            مَآ ءَامَنَتْ قَبْلَهُم مِّن قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَٰهَآ ۖ أَفَهُمْ يُؤْمِنُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="6">
            Not a ˹single˺ society We destroyed before them ever believed ˹after receiving the signs˺. Will these ˹pagans˺ then believe?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="7">
            وَمَآ أَرْسَلْنَا قَبْلَكَ إِلَّا رِجَالًۭا نُّوحِىٓ إِلَيْهِمْ ۖ فَسْـَٔلُوٓا۟ أَهْلَ ٱلذِّكْرِ إِن كُنتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="7">
            We did not send ˹messengers˺ before you ˹O Prophet˺ except mere men inspired by Us. If you ˹polytheists˺ do not know ˹this already˺, then ask those who have knowledge ˹of the Scriptures˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            وَمَا جَعَلْنَٰهُمْ جَسَدًۭا لَّا يَأْكُلُونَ ٱلطَّعَامَ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ خَٰلِدِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">
            We did not give those messengers ˹supernatural˺ bodies that did not need food, nor were they immortal.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            ثُمَّ صَدَقْنَٰهُمُ ٱلْوَعْدَ فَأَنجَيْنَٰهُمْ وَمَن نَّشَآءُ وَأَهْلَكْنَا ٱلْمُسْرِفِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            Then We fulfilled Our promise to them, saving them along with whoever We willed and destroying the transgressors.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            لَقَدْ أَنزَلْنَآ إِلَيْكُمْ كِتَٰبًۭا فِيهِ ذِكْرُكُمْ ۖ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">
            We have surely revealed to you a Book, in which there is glory for you. Will you not then understand?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            وَكَمْ قَصَمْنَا مِن قَرْيَةٍۢ كَانَتْ ظَالِمَةًۭ وَأَنشَأْنَا بَعْدَهَا قَوْمًا ءَاخَرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            ˹Imagine˺ how many societies of wrongdoers We have destroyed, raising up other people after them!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            فَلَمَّآ أَحَسُّوا۟ بَأْسَنَآ إِذَا هُم مِّنْهَا يَرْكُضُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            When the wrongdoers sensed ˹the arrival of˺ Our torment, they started to run away from their cities.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            لَا تَرْكُضُوا۟ وَٱرْجِعُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰ مَآ أُتْرِفْتُمْ فِيهِ وَمَسَٰكِنِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُسْـَٔلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            ˹They were told,˺ “Do not run away! Return to your luxuries and your homes, so you may be questioned ˹about your fate˺.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            قَالُوا۟ يَٰوَيْلَنَآ إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَٰلِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            They cried, “Woe to us! We have surely been wrongdoers.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            فَمَا زَالَت تِّلْكَ دَعْوَىٰهُمْ حَتَّىٰ جَعَلْنَٰهُمْ حَصِيدًا خَٰمِدِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">
            They kept repeating their cry until We mowed them down, ˹leaving them˺ lifeless.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            وَمَا خَلَقْنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا لَٰعِبِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="16">
            We did not create the heavens and the earth and everything in between for sport.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            لَوْ أَرَدْنَآ أَن نَّتَّخِذَ لَهْوًۭا لَّٱتَّخَذْنَٰهُ مِن لَّدُنَّآ إِن كُنَّا فَٰعِلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            Had We intended to take ˹some˺ amusement, We could have found it in Our presence, if that had been Our Will.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            بَلْ نَقْذِفُ بِٱلْحَقِّ عَلَى ٱلْبَٰطِلِ فَيَدْمَغُهُۥ فَإِذَا هُوَ زَاهِقٌۭ ۚ وَلَكُمُ ٱلْوَيْلُ مِمَّا تَصِفُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="18">
            In fact, We hurl the truth against falsehood, leaving it crushed, and it quickly vanishes. And woe be to you for what you claim!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            وَلَهُۥ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ وَمَنْ عِندَهُۥ لَا يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ عَنْ عِبَادَتِهِۦ وَلَا يَسْتَحْسِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            To Him belong all those in the heavens and the earth. And those nearest to Him are not too proud to worship Him, nor do they tire.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            يُسَبِّحُونَ ٱلَّيْلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ لَا يَفْتُرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            They glorify ˹Him˺ day and night, never wavering.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            أَمِ ٱتَّخَذُوٓا۟ ءَالِهَةًۭ مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ هُمْ يُنشِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            Or have they taken gods from the earth, who can raise the dead?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">
            لَوْ كَانَ فِيهِمَآ ءَالِهَةٌ إِلَّا ٱللَّهُ لَفَسَدَتَا ۚ فَسُبْحَٰنَ ٱللَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَرْشِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="22">
            Had there been other gods besides Allah in the heavens or the earth, both ˹realms˺ would have surely been corrupted. So Glorified is Allah, Lord of the Throne, far above what they claim.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="23">
            لَا يُسْـَٔلُ عَمَّا يَفْعَلُ وَهُمْ يُسْـَٔلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">
            He cannot be questioned about what He does, but they will ˹all˺ be questioned.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            أَمِ ٱتَّخَذُوا۟ مِن دُونِهِۦٓ ءَالِهَةًۭ ۖ قُلْ هَاتُوا۟ بُرْهَٰنَكُمْ ۖ هَٰذَا ذِكْرُ مَن مَّعِىَ وَذِكْرُ مَن قَبْلِى ۗ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ ٱلْحَقَّ ۖ فَهُم مُّعْرِضُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            Or have they taken other gods besides Him? Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Show ˹me˺ your proof. Here is ˹the Quran,˺ the Reminder for those with me; along with ˹earlier Scriptures,˺ the Reminder for those before me.” But most of them do not know the truth, so they turn away.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            وَمَآ أَرْسَلْنَا مِن قَبْلِكَ مِن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا نُوحِىٓ إِلَيْهِ أَنَّهُۥ لَآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّآ أَنَا۠ فَٱعْبُدُونِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            We never sent a messenger before you ˹O Prophet˺ without revealing to him: “There is no god ˹worthy of worship˺ except Me, so worship Me ˹alone˺.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            وَقَالُوا۟ ٱتَّخَذَ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنُ وَلَدًۭا ۗ سُبْحَٰنَهُۥ ۚ بَلْ عِبَادٌۭ مُّكْرَمُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            And they say, “The Most Compassionate has offspring!” Glory be to Him! In fact, those ˹angels˺ are only ˹His˺ honoured servants,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            لَا يَسْبِقُونَهُۥ بِٱلْقَوْلِ وَهُم بِأَمْرِهِۦ يَعْمَلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            who do not speak until He has spoken, ˹only˺ acting at His command.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ وَلَا يَشْفَعُونَ إِلَّا لِمَنِ ٱرْتَضَىٰ وَهُم مِّنْ خَشْيَتِهِۦ مُشْفِقُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            He ˹fully˺ knows what is ahead of them and what is behind them. They do not intercede except for whom He approves, and they tremble in awe of Him.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="29">
            ۞ وَمَن يَقُلْ مِنْهُمْ إِنِّىٓ إِلَٰهٌۭ مِّن دُونِهِۦ فَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِيهِ جَهَنَّمَ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِى ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="29">
            Whoever of them were to say, “I am a god besides Him,” they would be rewarded with Hell by Us. This is how We reward the wrongdoers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="30">
            أَوَلَمْ يَرَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ كَانَتَا رَتْقًۭا فَفَتَقْنَٰهُمَا ۖ وَجَعَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلْمَآءِ كُلَّ شَىْءٍ حَىٍّ ۖ أَفَلَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="30">
            Do the disbelievers not realize that the heavens and earth were ˹once˺ one mass then We split them apart? And We created from water every living thing. Will they not then believe?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="31">
            وَجَعَلْنَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ رَوَٰسِىَ أَن تَمِيدَ بِهِمْ وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا فِجَاجًۭا سُبُلًۭا لَّعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="31">
            And We have placed firm mountains upon the earth so it does not shake with them, and made in it broad pathways so they may find their way.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="32">
            وَجَعَلْنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ سَقْفًۭا مَّحْفُوظًۭا ۖ وَهُمْ عَنْ ءَايَٰتِهَا مُعْرِضُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="32">
            And We have made the sky a well-protected canopy, still they turn away from its signs.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="33">
            وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلَّيْلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ وَٱلشَّمْسَ وَٱلْقَمَرَ ۖ كُلٌّۭ فِى فَلَكٍۢ يَسْبَحُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="33">
            And He is the One Who created the day and the night, the sun and the moon—each travelling in an orbit.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="34">
            وَمَا جَعَلْنَا لِبَشَرٍۢ مِّن قَبْلِكَ ٱلْخُلْدَ ۖ أَفَإِي۟ن مِّتَّ فَهُمُ ٱلْخَٰلِدُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="34">
            We have not granted immortality to any human before you ˹O Prophet˺: so if you die, will they live forever?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="35">
            كُلُّ نَفْسٍۢ ذَآئِقَةُ ٱلْمَوْتِ ۗ وَنَبْلُوكُم بِٱلشَّرِّ وَٱلْخَيْرِ فِتْنَةًۭ ۖ وَإِلَيْنَا تُرْجَعُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="35">
            Every soul will taste death. And We test you ˹O humanity˺ with good and evil as a trial, then to Us you will ˹all˺ be returned.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="36">
            وَإِذَا رَءَاكَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوٓا۟ إِن يَتَّخِذُونَكَ إِلَّا هُزُوًا أَهَٰذَا ٱلَّذِى يَذْكُرُ ءَالِهَتَكُمْ وَهُم بِذِكْرِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ هُمْ كَٰفِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="36">
            When the disbelievers see you ˹O Prophet˺, they only make fun of you, ˹saying,˺ “Is this the one who speaks ˹ill˺ of your gods?” while they disbelieve at the mention of the Most Compassionate.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="37">
            خُلِقَ ٱلْإِنسَٰنُ مِنْ عَجَلٍۢ ۚ سَأُو۟رِيكُمْ ءَايَٰتِى فَلَا تَسْتَعْجِلُونِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="37">
            Humankind is made of haste. I will soon show you My signs, so do not ask Me to hasten them.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="38">
            وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَىٰ هَٰذَا ٱلْوَعْدُ إِن كُنتُمْ صَٰدِقِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="38">
            They ask ˹the believers˺, “When will this threat come to pass if what you say is true?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="39">
            لَوْ يَعْلَمُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ حِينَ لَا يَكُفُّونَ عَن وُجُوهِهِمُ ٱلنَّارَ وَلَا عَن ظُهُورِهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يُنصَرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="39">
            If only the disbelievers knew that a time will come when they will not be able to keep the Fire off their faces or backs, nor will they be helped.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="40">
            بَلْ تَأْتِيهِم بَغْتَةًۭ فَتَبْهَتُهُمْ فَلَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ رَدَّهَا وَلَا هُمْ يُنظَرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="40">
            In fact, the Hour will take them by surprise, leaving them stunned. So they will not be able to avert it, nor will it be delayed from them.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="41">
