        ---

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            فَلَمَّا جَآءَهَا نُودِىَ أَنۢ بُورِكَ مَن فِى ٱلنَّارِ وَمَنْ حَوْلَهَا وَسُبْحَٰنَ ٱللَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            يَٰمُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّهُۥٓ أَنَا ٱللَّهُ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            O  Moses! It is truly I. I am Allah—the Almighty, All-Wise.
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            وَأَلْقِ عَصَاكَ ۚ فَلَمَّا رَءَاهَا تَهْتَزُّ كَأَنَّهَا جَآنٌّۭ وَلَّىٰ مُدْبِرًۭا وَلَمْ يُعَقِّبْ ۚ يَٰمُوسَىٰ لَا تَخَفْ إِنِّى لَا يَخَافُ لَدَىَّ ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            إِلَّا مَن ظَلَمَ ثُمَّ بَدَّلَ حُسْنًۢا بَعْدَ سُوٓءٍۢ فَإِنِّى غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            ˹Fear is˺ only for those who do wrong. But if they later mend ˹their˺ evil ˹ways˺ with good, then I am certainly All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            وَأَدْخِلْ يَدَكَ فِى جَيْبِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَآءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوٓءٍۢ ۖ فِى تِسْعِ ءَايَٰتٍ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ وَقَوْمِهِۦٓ ۚ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ قَوْمًۭا فَٰسِقِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            Now put your hand through ˹the opening of˺ your collar, it will come out ˹shining˺ white, unblemished. They have truly been a rebellious people.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            فَلَمَّا جَآءَتْهُمْ ءَايَٰتُنَا مُبْصِرَةًۭ قَالُوا۟ هَٰذَا سِحْرٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            But when Our enlightening signs came to them, they said, “This is pure magic.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            وَجَحَدُوا۟ بِهَا وَٱسْتَيْقَنَتْهَآ أَنفُسُهُمْ ظُلْمًۭا وَعُلُوًّۭا ۚ فَٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُفْسِدِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            And, although their hearts were convinced the signs were true, they still denied them wrongfully and arrogantly. See then what was the end of the corruptors!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            وَلَقَدْ ءَاتَيْنَا دَاوُۥدَ وَسُلَيْمَٰنَ عِلْمًۭا ۖ وَقَالَا ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى فَضَّلَنَا عَلَىٰ كَثِيرٍۢ مِّنْ عِبَادِهِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            وَوَرِثَ سُلَيْمَٰنُ دَاوُۥدَ ۖ وَقَالَ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّاسُ عُلِّمْنَا مَنطِقَ ٱلطَّيْرِ وَأُوتِينَا مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍ ۖ إِنَّ هَٰذَا لَهُوَ ٱلْفَضْلُ ٱلْمُبِينُ
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            وَحُشِرَ لِسُلَيْمَٰنَ جُنُودُهُۥ مِنَ ٱلْجِنِّ وَٱلْإِنسِ وَٱلطَّيْرِ فَهُمْ يُوزَعُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            Solomon’s forces of jinn, humans, and birds were rallied for him, perfectly organized.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَتَوْا۟ عَلَىٰ وَادِ ٱلنَّمْلِ قَالَتْ نَمْلَةٌۭ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّمْلُ ٱدْخُلُوا۟ مَسَٰكِنَكُمْ لَا يَحْطِمَنَّكُمْ سُلَيْمَٰنُ وَجُنُودُهُۥ وَهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُونَ
            </Aside>

            And when they came across a valley of ants, an ant warned, “O ants! Go quickly into your homes so Solomon and his armies do not crush you, unknowingly.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">

            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            So Solomon smiled in amusement at her words, and prayed, “My Lord! Inspire me to ˹always˺ be thankful for Your favours which You have blessed me and my parents with, and to do good deeds that please you. Admit me, by Your mercy, into ˹the company of˺ Your righteous servants.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            وَتَفَقَّدَ ٱلطَّيْرَ فَقَالَ مَا لِىَ لَآ أَرَى ٱلْهُدْهُدَ أَمْ كَانَ مِنَ ٱلْغَآئِبِينَ
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---

            إِنِّى وَجَدتُّ ٱمْرَأَةًۭ تَمْلِكُهُمْ وَأُوتِيَتْ مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍۢ وَلَهَا عَرْشٌ عَظِيمٌۭ

            </Aside>

        ---

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            أَلَّا يَسْجُدُوا۟ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى يُخْرِجُ ٱلْخَبْءَ فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تُخْفُونَ وَمَا تُعْلِنُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="30">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="31">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="32">

            </Aside>

            She said, “O chiefs! Advise me in this matter of mine, for I would never make any decision without you.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="33">
            قَالُوا۟ نَحْنُ أُو۟لُوا۟ قُوَّةٍۢ وَأُو۟لُوا۟ بَأْسٍۢ شَدِيدٍۢ وَٱلْأَمْرُ إِلَيْكِ فَٱنظُرِى مَاذَا تَأْمُرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="33">
            They responded, “We are a people of strength and great ˹military˺ might, but the decision is yours, so decide what you will command.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="34">
            قَالَتْ إِنَّ ٱلْمُلُوكَ إِذَا دَخَلُوا۟ قَرْيَةً أَفْسَدُوهَا وَجَعَلُوٓا۟ أَعِزَّةَ أَهْلِهَآ أَذِلَّةًۭ ۖ وَكَذَٰلِكَ يَفْعَلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="34">
            She reasoned, “Indeed, when kings invade a land, they ruin it and debase its nobles. They really do so!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="35">
            وَإِنِّى مُرْسِلَةٌ إِلَيْهِم بِهَدِيَّةٍۢ فَنَاظِرَةٌۢ بِمَ يَرْجِعُ ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="35">
            But I will certainly send him a gift, and see what ˹response˺ my envoys will return with.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="36">
            فَلَمَّا جَآءَ سُلَيْمَٰنَ قَالَ أَتُمِدُّونَنِ بِمَالٍۢ فَمَآ ءَاتَىٰنِۦَ ٱللَّهُ خَيْرٌۭ مِّمَّآ ءَاتَىٰكُم بَلْ أَنتُم بِهَدِيَّتِكُمْ تَفْرَحُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="36">
            When the chief-envoy came to him, Solomon said, “Do you offer me wealth? What Allah has granted me is far greater than what He has granted you. No! It is you who rejoice in ˹receiving˺ gifts.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="37">
            ٱرْجِعْ إِلَيْهِمْ فَلَنَأْتِيَنَّهُم بِجُنُودٍۢ لَّا قِبَلَ لَهُم بِهَا وَلَنُخْرِجَنَّهُم مِّنْهَآ أَذِلَّةًۭ وَهُمْ صَٰغِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="37">
            Go back to them, for we will certainly mobilize against them forces which they can never resist, and we will drive them out from there in disgrace, fully humbled.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="38">
            قَالَ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْمَلَؤُا۟ أَيُّكُمْ يَأْتِينِى بِعَرْشِهَا قَبْلَ أَن يَأْتُونِى مُسْلِمِينَ
            </Aside>
