            <Aside type="quran" title="1">
            قُلْ أُوحِىَ إِلَىَّ أَنَّهُ ٱسْتَمَعَ نَفَرٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلْجِنِّ فَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا سَمِعْنَا قُرْءَانًا عَجَبًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="1">
            Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “It has been revealed to me that a group of jinn listened ˹to the Quran,˺ and said ˹to their fellow jinn˺: ‘Indeed, we have heard a wondrous recitation.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="2">
            يَهْدِىٓ إِلَى ٱلرُّشْدِ فَـَٔامَنَّا بِهِۦ ۖ وَلَن نُّشْرِكَ بِرَبِّنَآ أَحَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="2">
            It leads to Right Guidance so we believed in it, and we will never associate anyone with our Lord ˹in worship˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="3">
            وَأَنَّهُۥ تَعَٰلَىٰ جَدُّ رَبِّنَا مَا ٱتَّخَذَ صَٰحِبَةًۭ وَلَا وَلَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="3">
            ˹Now, we believe that˺ our Lord—Exalted is His Majesty—has neither taken a mate nor offspring,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="4">
            وَأَنَّهُۥ كَانَ يَقُولُ سَفِيهُنَا عَلَى ٱللَّهِ شَطَطًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="4">
            and that the foolish of us used to utter ˹outrageous˺ falsehoods about Allah.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="5">
            وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن تَقُولَ ٱلْإِنسُ وَٱلْجِنُّ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="5">
            We certainly thought that humans and jinn would never speak lies about Allah.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="6">
            وَأَنَّهُۥ كَانَ رِجَالٌۭ مِّنَ ٱلْإِنسِ يَعُوذُونَ بِرِجَالٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْجِنِّ فَزَادُوهُمْ رَهَقًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="6">
            And some men used to seek refuge with some jinn—so they increased each other in wickedness.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="7">
            وَأَنَّهُمْ ظَنُّوا۟ كَمَا ظَنَنتُمْ أَن لَّن يَبْعَثَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="7">
            And those ˹humans˺ thought, just like you ˹jinn˺, that Allah would not resurrect anyone ˹for judgment˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            وَأَنَّا لَمَسْنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ فَوَجَدْنَٰهَا مُلِئَتْ حَرَسًۭا شَدِيدًۭا وَشُهُبًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">
            ˹Earlier˺ we tried to reach heaven ˹for news˺, only to find it filled with stern guards and shooting stars.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            وَأَنَّا كُنَّا نَقْعُدُ مِنْهَا مَقَٰعِدَ لِلسَّمْعِ ۖ فَمَن يَسْتَمِعِ ٱلْءَانَ يَجِدْ لَهُۥ شِهَابًۭا رَّصَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            We used to take up positions there for eavesdropping, but whoever dares eavesdrop now will find a flare lying in wait for them.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            وَأَنَّا لَا نَدْرِىٓ أَشَرٌّ أُرِيدَ بِمَن فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ أَمْ أَرَادَ بِهِمْ رَبُّهُمْ رَشَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">
            Now, we have no clue whether evil is intended for those on earth, or their Lord intends for them what is right.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            وَأَنَّا مِنَّا ٱلصَّٰلِحُونَ وَمِنَّا دُونَ ذَٰلِكَ ۖ كُنَّا طَرَآئِقَ قِدَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            Among us are those who are righteous and those who are less so. We have been of different factions.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن نُّعْجِزَ ٱللَّهَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَن نُّعْجِزَهُۥ هَرَبًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            ˹Now,˺ we truly know that we cannot frustrate Allah on earth, nor can we escape from Him ˹into heaven˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            وَأَنَّا لَمَّا سَمِعْنَا ٱلْهُدَىٰٓ ءَامَنَّا بِهِۦ ۖ فَمَن يُؤْمِنۢ بِرَبِّهِۦ فَلَا يَخَافُ بَخْسًۭا وَلَا رَهَقًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            When we heard the guidance ˹of the Quran˺, we ˹readily˺ believed in it. For whoever believes in their Lord will have no fear of being denied ˹a reward˺ or wronged.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            وَأَنَّا مِنَّا ٱلْمُسْلِمُونَ وَمِنَّا ٱلْقَٰسِطُونَ ۖ فَمَنْ أَسْلَمَ فَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ تَحَرَّوْا۟ رَشَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            And among us are those who have submitted ˹to Allah˺ and those who are deviant. So ˹as for˺ those who submitted, it is they who have attained Right Guidance.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            وَأَمَّا ٱلْقَٰسِطُونَ فَكَانُوا۟ لِجَهَنَّمَ حَطَبًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">
            And as for the deviant, they will be fuel for Hell.’”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            وَأَلَّوِ ٱسْتَقَٰمُوا۟ عَلَى ٱلطَّرِيقَةِ لَأَسْقَيْنَٰهُم مَّآءً غَدَقًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="16">
            Had the deniers followed the Right Way, We would have certainly granted them abundant rain to drink—
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            لِّنَفْتِنَهُمْ فِيهِ ۚ وَمَن يُعْرِضْ عَن ذِكْرِ رَبِّهِۦ يَسْلُكْهُ عَذَابًۭا صَعَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            as a test for them. And whoever turns away from the remembrance of their Lord will be admitted by Him into an overwhelming punishment.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            وَأَنَّ ٱلْمَسَٰجِدَ لِلَّهِ فَلَا تَدْعُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="18">
            The places of worship are ˹only˺ for Allah, so do not invoke anyone besides Him.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            وَأَنَّهُۥ لَمَّا قَامَ عَبْدُ ٱللَّهِ يَدْعُوهُ كَادُوا۟ يَكُونُونَ عَلَيْهِ لِبَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            Yet when the servant of Allah stood up calling upon Him ˹alone˺, the pagans almost swarmed over him.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            قُلْ إِنَّمَآ أَدْعُوا۟ رَبِّى وَلَآ أُشْرِكُ بِهِۦٓ أَحَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “I call only upon my Lord, associating none with Him ˹in worship˺.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            قُلْ إِنِّى لَآ أَمْلِكُ لَكُمْ ضَرًّۭا وَلَا رَشَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            Say, “It is not in my power to harm or benefit you.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">
            قُلْ إِنِّى لَن يُجِيرَنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدٌۭ وَلَنْ أَجِدَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مُلْتَحَدًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="22">
            Say, “No one can protect me from Allah ˹if I were to disobey Him˺, nor can I find any refuge other than Him.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="23">
            إِلَّا بَلَٰغًۭا مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرِسَٰلَٰتِهِۦ ۚ وَمَن يَعْصِ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُۥ فَإِنَّ لَهُۥ نَارَ جَهَنَّمَ خَٰلِدِينَ فِيهَآ أَبَدًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">
            ˹My duty is˺ only to convey ˹the truth˺ from Allah and ˹deliver˺ His messages.” And whoever disobeys Allah and His Messenger will certainly be in the Fire of Hell, to stay there for ever and ever.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا رَأَوْا۟ مَا يُوعَدُونَ فَسَيَعْلَمُونَ مَنْ أَضْعَفُ نَاصِرًۭا وَأَقَلُّ عَدَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            Only when they see what they have been threatened with will they know who is weaker in helpers and inferior in manpower.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            قُلْ إِنْ أَدْرِىٓ أَقَرِيبٌۭ مَّا تُوعَدُونَ أَمْ يَجْعَلُ لَهُۥ رَبِّىٓ أَمَدًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            Say, “I do not know if what you are promised is near or my Lord has set a distant time for it.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            عَٰلِمُ ٱلْغَيْبِ فَلَا يُظْهِرُ عَلَىٰ غَيْبِهِۦٓ أَحَدًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            ˹He is the˺ Knower of the unseen, disclosing none of it to anyone,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            إِلَّا مَنِ ٱرْتَضَىٰ مِن رَّسُولٍۢ فَإِنَّهُۥ يَسْلُكُ مِنۢ بَيْنِ يَدَيْهِ وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِۦ رَصَدًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            except messengers of His choice. Then He appoints angel-guards before and behind them
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            لِّيَعْلَمَ أَن قَدْ أَبْلَغُوا۟ رِسَٰلَٰتِ رَبِّهِمْ وَأَحَاطَ بِمَا لَدَيْهِمْ وَأَحْصَىٰ كُلَّ شَىْءٍ عَدَدًۢا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            to ensure that the messengers fully deliver the messages of their Lord—though He ˹already˺ knows all about them, and keeps account of everything.”
            </Aside>
