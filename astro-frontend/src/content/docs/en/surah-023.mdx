            قَدْ أَفْلَحَ ٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ

            <Aside type="reference" title="1">
            Successful indeed are the believers:
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="2">
            ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ فِى صَلَاتِهِمْ خَٰشِعُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="2">
            those who humble themselves in prayer;
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="3">
            وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ عَنِ ٱللَّغْوِ مُعْرِضُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="3">
            those who avoid idle talk;
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="4">
            وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِلزَّكَوٰةِ فَٰعِلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="4">
            those who pay alms-tax;
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="5">
            وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِفُرُوجِهِمْ حَٰفِظُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="5">
            those who guard their chastity
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="6">
            إِلَّا عَلَىٰٓ أَزْوَٰجِهِمْ أَوْ مَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَٰنُهُمْ فَإِنَّهُمْ غَيْرُ مَلُومِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="6">
            except with their wives or those ˹bondwomen˺ in their possession, for then they are free from blame,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="7">
            فَمَنِ ٱبْتَغَىٰ وَرَآءَ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْعَادُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="7">
            but whoever seeks beyond that are the transgressors;
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِأَمَٰنَٰتِهِمْ وَعَهْدِهِمْ رَٰعُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">
            ˹the believers are also˺ those who are true to their trusts and covenants;
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ عَلَىٰ صَلَوَٰتِهِمْ يُحَافِظُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            and those who are ˹properly˺ observant of their prayers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْوَٰرِثُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">
            These are the ones who will be awarded
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            ٱلَّذِينَ يَرِثُونَ ٱلْفِرْدَوْسَ هُمْ فِيهَا خَٰلِدُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            Paradise as their own. They will be there forever.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ مِن سُلَٰلَةٍۢ مِّن طِينٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            And indeed, We created humankind from an extract of clay,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            ثُمَّ جَعَلْنَٰهُ نُطْفَةًۭ فِى قَرَارٍۢ مَّكِينٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            then placed each ˹human˺ as a sperm-drop in a secure place,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            ثُمَّ خَلَقْنَا ٱلنُّطْفَةَ عَلَقَةًۭ فَخَلَقْنَا ٱلْعَلَقَةَ مُضْغَةًۭ فَخَلَقْنَا ٱلْمُضْغَةَ عِظَٰمًۭا فَكَسَوْنَا ٱلْعِظَٰمَ لَحْمًۭا ثُمَّ أَنشَأْنَٰهُ خَلْقًا ءَاخَرَ ۚ فَتَبَارَكَ ٱللَّهُ أَحْسَنُ ٱلْخَٰلِقِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            then We developed the drop into a clinging clot ˹of blood˺, then developed the clot into a lump ˹of flesh˺, then developed the lump into bones, then clothed the bones with flesh, then We brought it into being as a new creation. So Blessed is Allah, the Best of Creators.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            ثُمَّ إِنَّكُم بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ لَمَيِّتُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">
            After that you will surely die,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            ثُمَّ إِنَّكُمْ يَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَٰمَةِ تُبْعَثُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="16">
            then on the Day of Judgment you will be resurrected.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا فَوْقَكُمْ سَبْعَ طَرَآئِقَ وَمَا كُنَّا عَنِ ٱلْخَلْقِ غَٰفِلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            And indeed, We created above you seven levels ˹of heaven˺. We are never unmindful of ˹Our˺ creation.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            وَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۢ بِقَدَرٍۢ فَأَسْكَنَّٰهُ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰ ذَهَابٍۭ بِهِۦ لَقَٰدِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="18">
            We send down rain from the sky in perfect measure, causing it to soak into the earth. And We are surely able to take it away.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            فَأَنشَأْنَا لَكُم بِهِۦ جَنَّٰتٍۢ مِّن نَّخِيلٍۢ وَأَعْنَٰبٍۢ لَّكُمْ فِيهَا فَوَٰكِهُ كَثِيرَةٌۭ وَمِنْهَا تَأْكُلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            With it We produce for you gardens of palm trees and grapevines, in which there are abundant fruits, and from which you may eat,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            وَشَجَرَةًۭ تَخْرُجُ مِن طُورِ سَيْنَآءَ تَنۢبُتُ بِٱلدُّهْنِ وَصِبْغٍۢ لِّلْءَاكِلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            as well as ˹olive˺ trees which grow at Mount Sinai, providing oil and a condiment to eat.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            وَإِنَّ لَكُمْ فِى ٱلْأَنْعَٰمِ لَعِبْرَةًۭ ۖ نُّسْقِيكُم مِّمَّا فِى بُطُونِهَا وَلَكُمْ فِيهَا مَنَٰفِعُ كَثِيرَةٌۭ وَمِنْهَا تَأْكُلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            And there is certainly a lesson for you in cattle, from whose bellies We give you ˹milk˺ to drink, and in them are many other benefits for you, and from them you may eat.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">
            وَعَلَيْهَا وَعَلَى ٱلْفُلْكِ تُحْمَلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="22">
            And you are carried upon ˹some of˺ them and upon ships.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="23">
            وَلَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦ فَقَالَ يَٰقَوْمِ ٱعْبُدُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ مَا لَكُم مِّنْ إِلَٰهٍ غَيْرُهُۥٓ ۖ أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">
            Indeed, We sent Noah to his people. He declared, “O my people! Worship Allah ˹alone˺. You have no god other than Him. Will you not then fear ˹Him˺?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            فَقَالَ ٱلْمَلَؤُا۟ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن قَوْمِهِۦ مَا هَٰذَآ إِلَّا بَشَرٌۭ مِّثْلُكُمْ يُرِيدُ أَن يَتَفَضَّلَ عَلَيْكُمْ وَلَوْ شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ لَأَنزَلَ مَلَٰٓئِكَةًۭ مَّا سَمِعْنَا بِهَٰذَا فِىٓ ءَابَآئِنَا ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            But the disbelieving chiefs of his people said ˹to the masses˺, “This is only a human like you, who wants to be superior to you. Had Allah willed, He could have easily sent down angels ˹instead˺. We have never heard of this in ˹the history of˺ our forefathers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا رَجُلٌۢ بِهِۦ جِنَّةٌۭ فَتَرَبَّصُوا۟ بِهِۦ حَتَّىٰ حِينٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            He is simply insane, so bear with him for a while.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            قَالَ رَبِّ ٱنصُرْنِى بِمَا كَذَّبُونِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            Noah prayed, “My Lord! Help me, because they have denied ˹me˺.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            فَأَوْحَيْنَآ إِلَيْهِ أَنِ ٱصْنَعِ ٱلْفُلْكَ بِأَعْيُنِنَا وَوَحْيِنَا فَإِذَا جَآءَ أَمْرُنَا وَفَارَ ٱلتَّنُّورُ ۙ فَٱسْلُكْ فِيهَا مِن كُلٍّۢ زَوْجَيْنِ ٱثْنَيْنِ وَأَهْلَكَ إِلَّا مَن سَبَقَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلْقَوْلُ مِنْهُمْ ۖ وَلَا تُخَٰطِبْنِى فِى ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوٓا۟ ۖ إِنَّهُم مُّغْرَقُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            So We inspired him: “Build the Ark under Our ˹watchful˺ Eyes and directions. Then when Our command comes and the oven bursts ˹with water˺, take on board a pair from every species along with your family—except those against whom the decree ˹to drown˺ has already been passed. And do not plead with Me for those who have done wrong, for they will surely be drowned.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            فَإِذَا ٱسْتَوَيْتَ أَنتَ وَمَن مَّعَكَ عَلَى ٱلْفُلْكِ فَقُلِ ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى نَجَّىٰنَا مِنَ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            Then when you and those with you have settled in the Ark, say, “All praise is for Allah, Who saved us from the wrongdoing people.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="29">
            وَقُل رَّبِّ أَنزِلْنِى مُنزَلًۭا مُّبَارَكًۭا وَأَنتَ خَيْرُ ٱلْمُنزِلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="29">
            And pray, “My Lord! Allow me a blessed landing, for You are the best accommodator.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="30">
            إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَءَايَٰتٍۢ وَإِن كُنَّا لَمُبْتَلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="30">
            Surely in this are lessons. And We ˹always˺ put ˹people˺ to the test.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="31">
            ثُمَّ أَنشَأْنَا مِنۢ بَعْدِهِمْ قَرْنًا ءَاخَرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="31">
            Then We raised another generation after them,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="32">
            فَأَرْسَلْنَا فِيهِمْ رَسُولًۭا مِّنْهُمْ أَنِ ٱعْبُدُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ مَا لَكُم مِّنْ إِلَٰهٍ غَيْرُهُۥٓ ۖ أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="32">
            and sent to them a messenger from among themselves, ˹declaring,˺ “Worship Allah ˹alone˺. You have no god other than Him. Will you not then fear ˹Him˺?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="33">
            وَقَالَ ٱلْمَلَأُ مِن قَوْمِهِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَكَذَّبُوا۟ بِلِقَآءِ ٱلْءَاخِرَةِ وَأَتْرَفْنَٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنْيَا مَا هَٰذَآ إِلَّا بَشَرٌۭ مِّثْلُكُمْ يَأْكُلُ مِمَّا تَأْكُلُونَ مِنْهُ وَيَشْرَبُ مِمَّا تَشْرَبُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="33">
            But the chiefs of his people—who disbelieved, denied the meeting ˹with Allah˺ in the Hereafter, and were spoiled by the worldly luxuries We had provided for them—said ˹to the masses˺, “This is only a human like you. He eats what you eat, and drinks what you drink.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="34">
            وَلَئِنْ أَطَعْتُم بَشَرًۭا مِّثْلَكُمْ إِنَّكُمْ إِذًۭا لَّخَٰسِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="34">
            And if you ˹ever˺ obey a human like yourselves, then you would certainly be losers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="35">
            أَيَعِدُكُمْ أَنَّكُمْ إِذَا مِتُّمْ وَكُنتُمْ تُرَابًۭا وَعِظَٰمًا أَنَّكُم مُّخْرَجُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="35">
            Does he promise you that once you are dead and reduced to dust and bones, you will be brought forth ˹alive˺?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="36">
            ۞ هَيْهَاتَ هَيْهَاتَ لِمَا تُوعَدُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="36">
            Impossible, simply impossible is what you are promised!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="37">
            إِنْ هِىَ إِلَّا حَيَاتُنَا ٱلدُّنْيَا نَمُوتُ وَنَحْيَا وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمَبْعُوثِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="37">
            There is nothing beyond our worldly life. We die, others are born, and none will be resurrected.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="38">
            إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا رَجُلٌ ٱفْتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًۭا وَمَا نَحْنُ لَهُۥ بِمُؤْمِنِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="38">
            He is no more than a man who has fabricated a lie about Allah, and we will never believe in him.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="39">
            قَالَ رَبِّ ٱنصُرْنِى بِمَا كَذَّبُونِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="39">
            The messenger prayed, “My Lord! Help me, because they have denied ˹me˺.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="40">
            قَالَ عَمَّا قَلِيلٍۢ لَّيُصْبِحُنَّ نَٰدِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="40">
            Allah responded, “Soon they will be truly regretful.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="41">
            فَأَخَذَتْهُمُ ٱلصَّيْحَةُ بِٱلْحَقِّ فَجَعَلْنَٰهُمْ غُثَآءًۭ ۚ فَبُعْدًۭا لِّلْقَوْمِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="41">
            Then the ˹mighty˺ blast overtook them with justice, and We reduced them to rubble. So away with the wrongdoing people!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="42">
            ثُمَّ أَنشَأْنَا مِنۢ بَعْدِهِمْ قُرُونًا ءَاخَرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="42">
            Then We raised other generations after them.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="43">
            مَا تَسْبِقُ مِنْ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلَهَا وَمَا يَسْتَـْٔخِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="43">
            No people can advance their doom, nor can they delay it.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="44">
            ثُمَّ أَرْسَلْنَا رُسُلَنَا تَتْرَا ۖ كُلَّ مَا جَآءَ أُمَّةًۭ رَّسُولُهَا كَذَّبُوهُ ۚ فَأَتْبَعْنَا بَعْضَهُم بَعْضًۭا وَجَعَلْنَٰهُمْ أَحَادِيثَ ۚ فَبُعْدًۭا لِّقَوْمٍۢ لَّا يُؤْمِنُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="44">
            Then We sent Our messengers in succession: whenever a messenger came to his people, they denied him. So We destroyed them, one after the other, reducing them to ˹cautionary˺ tales. So away with the people who refuse to believe!
            </Aside>
