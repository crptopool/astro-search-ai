            <Aside type="quran" title="1">
            الٓر ۚ تِلْكَ ءَايَٰتُ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ وَقُرْءَانٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="1">
            Alif-Lãm-Ra. These are the verses of the Book; the clear Quran.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="2">
            رُّبَمَا يَوَدُّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لَوْ كَانُوا۟ مُسْلِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="2">
            ˹The day will come when˺ the disbelievers will certainly wish they had submitted ˹to Allah˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="3">
            ذَرْهُمْ يَأْكُلُوا۟ وَيَتَمَتَّعُوا۟ وَيُلْهِهِمُ ٱلْأَمَلُ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَعْلَمُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="3">
            ˹So˺ let them eat and enjoy themselves and be diverted by ˹false˺ hope, for they will soon know.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="4">
            وَمَآ أَهْلَكْنَا مِن قَرْيَةٍ إِلَّا وَلَهَا كِتَابٌۭ مَّعْلُومٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="4">
            We have never destroyed a society without a destined term.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="5">
            مَّا تَسْبِقُ مِنْ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلَهَا وَمَا يَسْتَـْٔخِرُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="5">
            No people can advance their doom, nor can they delay it.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="6">
            وَقَالُوا۟ يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِى نُزِّلَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلذِّكْرُ إِنَّكَ لَمَجْنُونٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="6">
            They say, “O you to whom the Reminder is revealed! You must be insane!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="7">
            لَّوْ مَا تَأْتِينَا بِٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةِ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّٰدِقِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="7">
            Why do you not bring us the angels, if what you say is true?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            مَا نُنَزِّلُ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةَ إِلَّا بِٱلْحَقِّ وَمَا كَانُوٓا۟ إِذًۭا مُّنظَرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">
            We do not send the angels down except for a just cause, and then ˹the end of˺ the disbelievers will not be delayed.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَزَّلْنَا ٱلذِّكْرَ وَإِنَّا لَهُۥ لَحَٰفِظُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            It is certainly We Who have revealed the Reminder, and it is certainly We Who will preserve it.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            وَلَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا مِن قَبْلِكَ فِى شِيَعِ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">
            Indeed, We sent messengers before you ˹O Prophet˺ among the groups of early peoples,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            وَمَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            but no messenger ever came to them without being mocked.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            كَذَٰلِكَ نَسْلُكُهُۥ فِى قُلُوبِ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            This is how We allow disbelief ˹to steep˺ into the hearts of the wicked.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ بِهِۦ ۖ وَقَدْ خَلَتْ سُنَّةُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            They would not believe in this ˹Quran˺ despite the ˹many˺ examples of those ˹destroyed˺ before.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            وَلَوْ فَتَحْنَا عَلَيْهِم بَابًۭا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَظَلُّوا۟ فِيهِ يَعْرُجُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            And even if We opened for them a gate to heaven, through which they continued to ascend,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            لَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّمَا سُكِّرَتْ أَبْصَٰرُنَا بَلْ نَحْنُ قَوْمٌۭ مَّسْحُورُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">
            still they would say, “Our eyes have truly been dazzled! In fact, we must have been bewitched.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            وَلَقَدْ جَعَلْنَا فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ بُرُوجًۭا وَزَيَّنَّٰهَا لِلنَّٰظِرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="16">
            Indeed, We have placed constellations in the sky, and adorned it for all to see.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            وَحَفِظْنَٰهَا مِن كُلِّ شَيْطَٰنٍۢ رَّجِيمٍ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            And We protected it from every accursed devil,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            إِلَّا مَنِ ٱسْتَرَقَ ٱلسَّمْعَ فَأَتْبَعَهُۥ شِهَابٌۭ مُّبِينٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="18">
            except the one eavesdropping, who is then pursued by a visible flare.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            وَٱلْأَرْضَ مَدَدْنَٰهَا وَأَلْقَيْنَا فِيهَا رَوَٰسِىَ وَأَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍۢ مَّوْزُونٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            As for the earth, We spread it out and placed upon it firm mountains, and caused everything to grow there in perfect balance.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            وَجَعَلْنَا لَكُمْ فِيهَا مَعَٰيِشَ وَمَن لَّسْتُمْ لَهُۥ بِرَٰزِقِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            And We made in it means of sustenance for you and others, who you do not provide for.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            وَإِن مِّن شَىْءٍ إِلَّا عِندَنَا خَزَآئِنُهُۥ وَمَا نُنَزِّلُهُۥٓ إِلَّا بِقَدَرٍۢ مَّعْلُومٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            There is not any means ˹of sustenance˺ whose reserves We do not hold, only bringing it forth in precise measure.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">
            وَأَرْسَلْنَا ٱلرِّيَٰحَ لَوَٰقِحَ فَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۭ فَأَسْقَيْنَٰكُمُوهُ وَمَآ أَنتُمْ لَهُۥ بِخَٰزِنِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="22">
            We send fertilizing winds, and bring down rain from the sky for you to drink. It is not you who hold its reserves.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="23">
            وَإِنَّا لَنَحْنُ نُحْىِۦ وَنُمِيتُ وَنَحْنُ ٱلْوَٰرِثُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">
            Surely it is We Who give life and cause death. And We are the ˹Eternal˺ Successor.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            وَلَقَدْ عَلِمْنَا ٱلْمُسْتَقْدِمِينَ مِنكُمْ وَلَقَدْ عَلِمْنَا ٱلْمُسْتَـْٔخِرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            We certainly know those who have gone before you and those who will come after ˹you˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ يَحْشُرُهُمْ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ حَكِيمٌ عَلِيمٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            Surely your Lord ˹alone˺ will gather them together ˹for judgment˺. He is truly All-Wise, All-Knowing.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَٰنَ مِن صَلْصَٰلٍۢ مِّنْ حَمَإٍۢ مَّسْنُونٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            Indeed, We created man from sounding clay moulded from black mud.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            وَٱلْجَآنَّ خَلَقْنَٰهُ مِن قَبْلُ مِن نَّارِ ٱلسَّمُومِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            As for the jinn, We created them earlier from smokeless fire.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            وَإِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةِ إِنِّى خَٰلِقٌۢ بَشَرًۭا مِّن صَلْصَٰلٍۢ مِّنْ حَمَإٍۢ مَّسْنُونٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            ˹Remember, O  Prophet˺ when your Lord said to the angels, “I am going to create a human being from sounding clay moulded from black mud.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="29">
            فَإِذَا سَوَّيْتُهُۥ وَنَفَخْتُ فِيهِ مِن رُّوحِى فَقَعُوا۟ لَهُۥ سَٰجِدِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="29">
            So when I have fashioned him and had a spirit of My Own ˹creation˺ breathed into him, fall down in prostration to him.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="30">
            فَسَجَدَ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ كُلُّهُمْ أَجْمَعُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="30">
            So the angels prostrated all together—
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="31">
            إِلَّآ إِبْلِيسَ أَبَىٰٓ أَن يَكُونَ مَعَ ٱلسَّٰجِدِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="31">
            but not Iblis, who refused to prostrate with the others.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="32">
            قَالَ يَٰٓإِبْلِيسُ مَا لَكَ أَلَّا تَكُونَ مَعَ ٱلسَّٰجِدِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="32">
            Allah asked, “O Iblis! What is the matter with you that you did not join others in prostration?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="33">
            قَالَ لَمْ أَكُن لِّأَسْجُدَ لِبَشَرٍ خَلَقْتَهُۥ مِن صَلْصَٰلٍۢ مِّنْ حَمَإٍۢ مَّسْنُونٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="33">
            He replied, “It is not for me to prostrate to a human You created from sounding clay moulded from black mud.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="34">
            قَالَ فَٱخْرُجْ مِنْهَا فَإِنَّكَ رَجِيمٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="34">
            Allah commanded, “Then get out of Paradise, for you are truly cursed.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="35">
            وَإِنَّ عَلَيْكَ ٱللَّعْنَةَ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="35">
            And surely upon you is condemnation until the Day of Judgment.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="36">
            قَالَ رَبِّ فَأَنظِرْنِىٓ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ يُبْعَثُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="36">
            Satan appealed, “My Lord! Then delay my end until the Day of their resurrection.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="37">
            قَالَ فَإِنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلْمُنظَرِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="37">
            Allah said, “You will be delayed
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="38">
            إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ ٱلْوَقْتِ ٱلْمَعْلُومِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="38">
            until the appointed Day.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="39">
            قَالَ رَبِّ بِمَآ أَغْوَيْتَنِى لَأُزَيِّنَنَّ لَهُمْ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَأُغْوِيَنَّهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="39">
            Satan responded, “My Lord! For allowing me to stray I will surely tempt them on earth and mislead them all together,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="40">
            إِلَّا عِبَادَكَ مِنْهُمُ ٱلْمُخْلَصِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="40">
            except Your chosen servants among them.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="41">
            قَالَ هَٰذَا صِرَٰطٌ عَلَىَّ مُسْتَقِيمٌ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="41">
            Allah said, “This is the Way, binding on Me:
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="42">
            إِنَّ عِبَادِى لَيْسَ لَكَ عَلَيْهِمْ سُلْطَٰنٌ إِلَّا مَنِ ٱتَّبَعَكَ مِنَ ٱلْغَاوِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="42">
            you will certainly have no authority over My servants, except the deviant who follow you,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="43">
            وَإِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ لَمَوْعِدُهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="43">
            and surely Hell is their destined place, all together.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="44">
            لَهَا سَبْعَةُ أَبْوَٰبٍۢ لِّكُلِّ بَابٍۢ مِّنْهُمْ جُزْءٌۭ مَّقْسُومٌ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="44">
            It has seven gates, to each a group of them is designated.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="45">
            إِنَّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ فِى جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَعُيُونٍ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="45">
            Indeed, the righteous will be amid Gardens and springs.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="46">
            ٱدْخُلُوهَا بِسَلَٰمٍ ءَامِنِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="46">
            ˹It will be said to them,˺ “Enter in peace and security.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="47">
            وَنَزَعْنَا مَا فِى صُدُورِهِم مِّنْ غِلٍّ إِخْوَٰنًا عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍۢ مُّتَقَٰبِلِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="47">
            We will remove whatever bitterness they had in their hearts. In a friendly manner, they will be on thrones, facing one another.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="48">
            لَا يَمَسُّهُمْ فِيهَا نَصَبٌۭ وَمَا هُم مِّنْهَا بِمُخْرَجِينَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="48">
            No fatigue will touch them there, nor will they ever be asked to leave.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="49">
            ۞ نَبِّئْ عِبَادِىٓ أَنِّىٓ أَنَا ٱلْغَفُورُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="49">
            Inform My servants ˹O Prophet˺ that I am truly the All-Forgiving, Most Merciful,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="50">
            وَأَنَّ عَذَابِى هُوَ ٱلْعَذَابُ ٱلْأَلِيمُ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="50">
            and that My torment is indeed the most painful.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="51">
            وَنَبِّئْهُمْ عَن ضَيْفِ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="51">
            And inform them ˹O Prophet˺ about Abraham’s guests
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="52">
            إِذْ دَخَلُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ فَقَالُوا۟ سَلَٰمًۭا قَالَ إِنَّا مِنكُمْ وَجِلُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="52">
            who entered upon him and greeted ˹him with˺, “Peace!” He ˹later˺ said, “Surely we are afraid of you.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="53">
            قَالُوا۟ لَا تَوْجَلْ إِنَّا نُبَشِّرُكَ بِغُلَٰمٍ عَلِيمٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="53">
            They reassured ˹him˺, “Do not be afraid! Surely we give you good news of a knowledgeable son.”
            </Aside>
