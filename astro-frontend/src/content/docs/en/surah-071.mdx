            <Aside type="quran" title="1">
            إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦٓ أَنْ أَنذِرْ قَوْمَكَ مِن قَبْلِ أَن يَأْتِيَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="1">
            Indeed, We sent Noah to his people ˹saying to him˺, “Warn your people before a painful punishment comes to them.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="2">
            قَالَ يَٰقَوْمِ إِنِّى لَكُمْ نَذِيرٌۭ مُّبِينٌ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="2">
            Noah proclaimed, “O my people! I am truly sent to you with a clear warning:
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="3">
            أَنِ ٱعْبُدُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَٱتَّقُوهُ وَأَطِيعُونِ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="3">
            worship Allah ˹alone˺, fear Him, and obey me.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="4">
            يَغْفِرْ لَكُم مِّن ذُنُوبِكُمْ وَيُؤَخِّرْكُمْ إِلَىٰٓ أَجَلٍۢ مُّسَمًّى ۚ إِنَّ أَجَلَ ٱللَّهِ إِذَا جَآءَ لَا يُؤَخَّرُ ۖ لَوْ كُنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="4">
            He will forgive your sins, and delay your end until the appointed time. Indeed, when the time set by Allah comes, it cannot be delayed, if only you knew!”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="5">
            قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّى دَعَوْتُ قَوْمِى لَيْلًۭا وَنَهَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="5">
            He cried, “My Lord! I have surely called my people day and night,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="6">
            فَلَمْ يَزِدْهُمْ دُعَآءِىٓ إِلَّا فِرَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="6">
            but my calls only made them run farther away.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="7">
            وَإِنِّى كُلَّمَا دَعَوْتُهُمْ لِتَغْفِرَ لَهُمْ جَعَلُوٓا۟ أَصَٰبِعَهُمْ فِىٓ ءَاذَانِهِمْ وَٱسْتَغْشَوْا۟ ثِيَابَهُمْ وَأَصَرُّوا۟ وَٱسْتَكْبَرُوا۟ ٱسْتِكْبَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="7">
            And whenever I invite them to be forgiven by You, they press their fingers into their ears, cover themselves with their clothes, persist ˹in denial˺, and act very arrogantly.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            ثُمَّ إِنِّى دَعَوْتُهُمْ جِهَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">
            Then I certainly called them openly,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            ثُمَّ إِنِّىٓ أَعْلَنتُ لَهُمْ وَأَسْرَرْتُ لَهُمْ إِسْرَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            then I surely preached to them publicly and privately,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            فَقُلْتُ ٱسْتَغْفِرُوا۟ رَبَّكُمْ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ غَفَّارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">
            saying, ‘Seek your Lord’s forgiveness, ˹for˺ He is truly Most Forgiving.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            يُرْسِلِ ٱلسَّمَآءَ عَلَيْكُم مِّدْرَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            He will shower you with abundant rain,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            وَيُمْدِدْكُم بِأَمْوَٰلٍۢ وَبَنِينَ وَيَجْعَل لَّكُمْ جَنَّٰتٍۢ وَيَجْعَل لَّكُمْ أَنْهَٰرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            supply you with wealth and children, and give you gardens as well as rivers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            مَّا لَكُمْ لَا تَرْجُونَ لِلَّهِ وَقَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            What is the matter with you that you are not in awe of the Majesty of Allah,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            وَقَدْ خَلَقَكُمْ أَطْوَارًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            when He truly created you in stages ˹of development˺?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            أَلَمْ تَرَوْا۟ كَيْفَ خَلَقَ ٱللَّهُ سَبْعَ سَمَٰوَٰتٍۢ طِبَاقًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">
            Do you not see how Allah created seven heavens, one above the other,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            وَجَعَلَ ٱلْقَمَرَ فِيهِنَّ نُورًۭا وَجَعَلَ ٱلشَّمْسَ سِرَاجًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="16">
            placing the moon within them as a ˹reflected˺ light, and the sun as a ˹radiant˺ lamp?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            وَٱللَّهُ أَنۢبَتَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ نَبَاتًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            Allah ˹alone˺ caused you to grow from the earth like a plant.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            ثُمَّ يُعِيدُكُمْ فِيهَا وَيُخْرِجُكُمْ إِخْرَاجًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="18">
            Then He will return you to it, and then simply bring you forth ˹again˺.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            وَٱللَّهُ جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلْأَرْضَ بِسَاطًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            And Allah ˹alone˺ spread out the earth for you
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            لِّتَسْلُكُوا۟ مِنْهَا سُبُلًۭا فِجَاجًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            to walk along its spacious pathways.’”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            قَالَ نُوحٌۭ رَّبِّ إِنَّهُمْ عَصَوْنِى وَٱتَّبَعُوا۟ مَن لَّمْ يَزِدْهُ مَالُهُۥ وَوَلَدُهُۥٓ إِلَّا خَسَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            ˹Eventually,˺ Noah cried, “My Lord! They have certainly persisted in disobeying me, and followed ˹instead˺ those ˹elite˺ whose ˹abundant˺ wealth and children only increase them in loss,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">
            وَمَكَرُوا۟ مَكْرًۭا كُبَّارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="22">
            and who have devised a tremendous plot,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="23">
            وَقَالُوا۟ لَا تَذَرُنَّ ءَالِهَتَكُمْ وَلَا تَذَرُنَّ وَدًّۭا وَلَا سُوَاعًۭا وَلَا يَغُوثَ وَيَعُوقَ وَنَسْرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">
            urging ˹their followers˺, ‘Do not abandon your idols—especially Wadd, Suwa’, Yaghûth, Ya’ûq, and Nasr.’
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            وَقَدْ أَضَلُّوا۟ كَثِيرًۭا ۖ وَلَا تَزِدِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ إِلَّا ضَلَٰلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            Those ˹elite˺ have already led many astray. So ˹O Lord˺, only allow the wrongdoers to stray farther away.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            مِّمَّا خَطِيٓـَٰٔتِهِمْ أُغْرِقُوا۟ فَأُدْخِلُوا۟ نَارًۭا فَلَمْ يَجِدُوا۟ لَهُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ أَنصَارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            So because of their sins, they were drowned, then admitted into the Fire. And they found none to help them against Allah.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            وَقَالَ نُوحٌۭ رَّبِّ لَا تَذَرْ عَلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ مِنَ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ دَيَّارًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            Noah had prayed, “My Lord! Do not leave a single disbeliever on earth.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            إِنَّكَ إِن تَذَرْهُمْ يُضِلُّوا۟ عِبَادَكَ وَلَا يَلِدُوٓا۟ إِلَّا فَاجِرًۭا كَفَّارًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            For if You spare ˹any of˺ them, they will certainly mislead Your servants, and give birth only to ˹wicked˺ sinners, staunch disbelievers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            رَّبِّ ٱغْفِرْ لِى وَلِوَٰلِدَىَّ وَلِمَن دَخَلَ بَيْتِىَ مُؤْمِنًۭا وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَٱلْمُؤْمِنَٰتِ وَلَا تَزِدِ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ إِلَّا تَبَارًۢا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            My Lord! Forgive me, my parents, and whoever enters my house in faith, and ˹all˺ believing men and women. And increase the wrongdoers only in destruction.”
            </Aside>

        ---
