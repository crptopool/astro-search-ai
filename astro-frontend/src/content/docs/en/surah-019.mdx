            </Aside>

            ˹The angels announced,˺ “O Zachariah! Indeed, We give you the good news of ˹the birth of˺ a son, whose name will be John—a name We have not given to anyone before.”
            </Aside>

        ---

            قَالَ رَبِّ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى غُلَٰمٌۭ وَكَانَتِ ٱمْرَأَتِى عَاقِرًۭا وَقَدْ بَلَغْتُ مِنَ ٱلْكِبَرِ عِتِيًّۭا

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            يَٰيَحْيَىٰ خُذِ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ بِقُوَّةٍۢ ۖ وَءَاتَيْنَٰهُ ٱلْحُكْمَ صَبِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            وَحَنَانًۭا مِّن لَّدُنَّا وَزَكَوٰةًۭ ۖ وَكَانَ تَقِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            as well as purity and compassion from Us. And he was God-fearing,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            وَبَرًّۢا بِوَٰلِدَيْهِ وَلَمْ يَكُن جَبَّارًا عَصِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            and kind to his parents. He was neither arrogant nor disobedient.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            وَسَلَٰمٌ عَلَيْهِ يَوْمَ وُلِدَ وَيَوْمَ يَمُوتُ وَيَوْمَ يُبْعَثُ حَيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---

            وَٱذْكُرْ فِى ٱلْكِتَٰبِ مَرْيَمَ إِذِ ٱنتَبَذَتْ مِنْ أَهْلِهَا مَكَانًۭا شَرْقِيًّۭا

            </Aside>

            قَالَتْ إِنِّىٓ أَعُوذُ بِٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ مِنكَ إِن كُنتَ تَقِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            She appealed, “I truly seek refuge in the Most Compassionate from you! ˹So leave me alone˺ if you are God-fearing.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            قَالَ إِنَّمَآ أَنَا۠ رَسُولُ رَبِّكِ لِأَهَبَ لَكِ غُلَٰمًۭا زَكِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            He responded, “I am only a messenger from your Lord, ˹sent˺ to bless you with a pure son.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            قَالَتْ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى غُلَٰمٌۭ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِى بَشَرٌۭ وَلَمْ أَكُ بَغِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            She wondered, “How can I have a son when no man has ever touched me, nor am I unchaste?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            قَالَ كَذَٰلِكِ قَالَ رَبُّكِ هُوَ عَلَىَّ هَيِّنٌۭ ۖ وَلِنَجْعَلَهُۥٓ ءَايَةًۭ لِّلنَّاسِ وَرَحْمَةًۭ مِّنَّا ۚ وَكَانَ أَمْرًۭا مَّقْضِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            He replied, “So will it be! Your Lord says, ‘It is easy for Me. And so will We make him a sign for humanity and a mercy from Us.’ It is a matter ˹already˺ decreed.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---

            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            فَنَادَىٰهَا مِن تَحْتِهَآ أَلَّا تَحْزَنِى قَدْ جَعَلَ رَبُّكِ تَحْتَكِ سَرِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            So a voice reassured her from below her, “Do not grieve! Your Lord has provided a stream at your feet.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            وَهُزِّىٓ إِلَيْكِ بِجِذْعِ ٱلنَّخْلَةِ تُسَٰقِطْ عَلَيْكِ رُطَبًۭا جَنِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            And shake the trunk of this palm tree towards you, it will drop fresh, ripe dates upon you.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            فَكُلِى وَٱشْرَبِى وَقَرِّى عَيْنًۭا ۖ فَإِمَّا تَرَيِنَّ مِنَ ٱلْبَشَرِ أَحَدًۭا فَقُولِىٓ إِنِّى نَذَرْتُ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ صَوْمًۭا فَلَنْ أُكَلِّمَ ٱلْيَوْمَ إِنسِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            So eat and drink, and put your heart at ease. But if you see any of the people, say, ‘I have vowed silence to the Most Compassionate, so I am not talking to anyone today.’”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            فَأَتَتْ بِهِۦ قَوْمَهَا تَحْمِلُهُۥ ۖ قَالُوا۟ يَٰمَرْيَمُ لَقَدْ جِئْتِ شَيْـًۭٔا فَرِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            Then she returned to her people, carrying him. They said ˹in shock˺, “O Mary! You have certainly done a horrible thing!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            يَٰٓأُخْتَ هَٰرُونَ مَا كَانَ أَبُوكِ ٱمْرَأَ سَوْءٍۢ وَمَا كَانَتْ أُمُّكِ بَغِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            O  sister of Aaron! Your father was not an indecent man, nor was your mother unchaste.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="29">
            فَأَشَارَتْ إِلَيْهِ ۖ قَالُوا۟ كَيْفَ نُكَلِّمُ مَن كَانَ فِى ٱلْمَهْدِ صَبِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="30">
            قَالَ إِنِّى عَبْدُ ٱللَّهِ ءَاتَىٰنِىَ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ وَجَعَلَنِى نَبِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="30">
            ˹Jesus˺ declared, “I am truly a servant of Allah. He has destined me to be given the Scripture and to be a prophet.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="31">
            وَجَعَلَنِى مُبَارَكًا أَيْنَ مَا كُنتُ وَأَوْصَٰنِى بِٱلصَّلَوٰةِ وَٱلزَّكَوٰةِ مَا دُمْتُ حَيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="31">
            He has made me a blessing wherever I go, and bid me to establish prayer and give alms-tax as long as I live,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="32">
            وَبَرًّۢا بِوَٰلِدَتِى وَلَمْ يَجْعَلْنِى جَبَّارًۭا شَقِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="32">
            and to be kind to my mother. He has not made me arrogant or defiant.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="33">
            وَٱلسَّلَٰمُ عَلَىَّ يَوْمَ وُلِدتُّ وَيَوْمَ أَمُوتُ وَيَوْمَ أُبْعَثُ حَيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="34">
            ذَٰلِكَ عِيسَى ٱبْنُ مَرْيَمَ ۚ قَوْلَ ٱلْحَقِّ ٱلَّذِى فِيهِ يَمْتَرُونَ
            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---

            </Aside>

            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="36">
            وَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ رَبِّى وَرَبُّكُمْ فَٱعْبُدُوهُ ۚ هَٰذَا صِرَٰطٌۭ مُّسْتَقِيمٌۭ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="36">
            ˹Jesus also declared,˺ “Surely Allah is my Lord and your Lord, so worship Him ˹alone˺. This is the Straight Path.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="37">
            فَٱخْتَلَفَ ٱلْأَحْزَابُ مِنۢ بَيْنِهِمْ ۖ فَوَيْلٌۭ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن مَّشْهَدِ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="37">
            Yet their ˹various˺ groups have differed among themselves ˹about him˺, so woe to the disbelievers when they face a tremendous Day!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="38">
            أَسْمِعْ بِهِمْ وَأَبْصِرْ يَوْمَ يَأْتُونَنَا ۖ لَٰكِنِ ٱلظَّٰلِمُونَ ٱلْيَوْمَ فِى ضَلَٰلٍۢ مُّبِينٍۢ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="38">
            How clearly will they hear and see on the Day they will come to Us! But today the wrongdoers are clearly astray.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="39">
            وَأَنذِرْهُمْ يَوْمَ ٱلْحَسْرَةِ إِذْ قُضِىَ ٱلْأَمْرُ وَهُمْ فِى غَفْلَةٍۢ وَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="39">
            And warn them ˹O Prophet˺ of the Day of Regret, when all matters will be settled, while they are ˹engrossed˺ in heedlessness and disbelief.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="40">
            إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَرِثُ ٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَنْ عَلَيْهَا وَإِلَيْنَا يُرْجَعُونَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="40">
            Indeed, it is We Who will succeed the earth and whoever is on it. And to Us they will ˹all˺ be returned.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="41">
            وَٱذْكُرْ فِى ٱلْكِتَٰبِ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ صِدِّيقًۭا نَّبِيًّا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="41">
            And mention in the Book ˹O Prophet, the story of˺ Abraham. He was surely a man of truth and a prophet.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="42">
            إِذْ قَالَ لِأَبِيهِ يَٰٓأَبَتِ لِمَ تَعْبُدُ مَا لَا يَسْمَعُ وَلَا يُبْصِرُ وَلَا يُغْنِى عَنكَ شَيْـًۭٔا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="42">
            ˹Remember˺ when he said to his father, “O dear father! Why do you worship what can neither hear nor see, nor benefit you at all?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="43">
            يَٰٓأَبَتِ إِنِّى قَدْ جَآءَنِى مِنَ ٱلْعِلْمِ مَا لَمْ يَأْتِكَ فَٱتَّبِعْنِىٓ أَهْدِكَ صِرَٰطًۭا سَوِيًّۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="43">
            O  dear father! I have certainly received some knowledge which you have not received, so follow me and I will guide you to the Straight Path.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="44">
