            <Aside type="quran" title="1">
            تَبَارَكَ ٱلَّذِى نَزَّلَ ٱلْفُرْقَانَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِۦ لِيَكُونَ لِلْعَٰلَمِينَ نَذِيرًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="1">
            Blessed is the One Who sent down the Standard
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="2">
            ٱلَّذِى لَهُۥ مُلْكُ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَمْ يَتَّخِذْ وَلَدًۭا وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُۥ شَرِيكٌۭ فِى ٱلْمُلْكِ وَخَلَقَ كُلَّ شَىْءٍۢ فَقَدَّرَهُۥ تَقْدِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="2">
            ˹Allah is˺ the One to Whom belongs the kingdom of the heavens and the earth, Who has never had ˹any˺ offspring, nor does He have a partner in ˹governing˺ the kingdom. He has created everything, ordaining it precisely.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="3">
            وَٱتَّخَذُوا۟ مِن دُونِهِۦٓ ءَالِهَةًۭ لَّا يَخْلُقُونَ شَيْـًۭٔا وَهُمْ يُخْلَقُونَ وَلَا يَمْلِكُونَ لِأَنفُسِهِمْ ضَرًّۭا وَلَا نَفْعًۭا وَلَا يَمْلِكُونَ مَوْتًۭا وَلَا حَيَوٰةًۭ وَلَا نُشُورًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="3">
            Yet they have taken besides Him gods who cannot create anything but are themselves created. Nor can they protect or benefit themselves. Nor can they control life, death, or resurrection.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="4">
            وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوٓا۟ إِنْ هَٰذَآ إِلَّآ إِفْكٌ ٱفْتَرَىٰهُ وَأَعَانَهُۥ عَلَيْهِ قَوْمٌ ءَاخَرُونَ ۖ فَقَدْ جَآءُو ظُلْمًۭا وَزُورًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="4">
            The disbelievers say, “This ˹Quran˺ is nothing but a fabrication which he made up with the help of others.” Their claim is totally unjustified and untrue!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="5">
            وَقَالُوٓا۟ أَسَٰطِيرُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ ٱكْتَتَبَهَا فَهِىَ تُمْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِ بُكْرَةًۭ وَأَصِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="5">
            And they say, “˹These revelations are only˺ ancient fables which he has had written down, and they are rehearsed to him morning and evening.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="6">
            قُلْ أَنزَلَهُ ٱلَّذِى يَعْلَمُ ٱلسِّرَّ فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ غَفُورًۭا رَّحِيمًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="6">
            Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “This ˹Quran˺ has been revealed by the One Who knows the secrets of the heavens and the earth. Surely He is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="7">
            وَقَالُوا۟ مَالِ هَٰذَا ٱلرَّسُولِ يَأْكُلُ ٱلطَّعَامَ وَيَمْشِى فِى ٱلْأَسْوَاقِ ۙ لَوْلَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْهِ مَلَكٌۭ فَيَكُونَ مَعَهُۥ نَذِيرًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="7">
            And they say ˹mockingly˺, “What kind of messenger is this who eats food and goes about in market-places ˹for a living˺? If only an angel had been sent down with him to be his co-warner,
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="8">
            أَوْ يُلْقَىٰٓ إِلَيْهِ كَنزٌ أَوْ تَكُونُ لَهُۥ جَنَّةٌۭ يَأْكُلُ مِنْهَا ۚ وَقَالَ ٱلظَّٰلِمُونَ إِن تَتَّبِعُونَ إِلَّا رَجُلًۭا مَّسْحُورًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="8">
            or a treasure had been cast down to him, or he had had a garden from which he may eat!” And the wrongdoers say ˹to the believers˺, “You are only following a bewitched man.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="9">
            ٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ ضَرَبُوا۟ لَكَ ٱلْأَمْثَٰلَ فَضَلُّوا۟ فَلَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ سَبِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="9">
            See ˹O Prophet˺ how they call you names! So they have gone so ˹far˺ astray that they cannot find the ˹Right˺ Way.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="10">
            تَبَارَكَ ٱلَّذِىٓ إِن شَآءَ جَعَلَ لَكَ خَيْرًۭا مِّن ذَٰلِكَ جَنَّٰتٍۢ تَجْرِى مِن تَحْتِهَا ٱلْأَنْهَٰرُ وَيَجْعَل لَّكَ قُصُورًۢا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="10">
            Blessed is the One Who—if He wills—can give you far better than ˹all˺ that: gardens under which rivers flow, and palaces as well.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="11">
            بَلْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِٱلسَّاعَةِ ۖ وَأَعْتَدْنَا لِمَن كَذَّبَ بِٱلسَّاعَةِ سَعِيرًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="11">
            In fact, they deny the Hour. And for the deniers of the Hour, We have prepared a blazing Fire.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="12">
            إِذَا رَأَتْهُم مِّن مَّكَانٍۭ بَعِيدٍۢ سَمِعُوا۟ لَهَا تَغَيُّظًۭا وَزَفِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="12">
            Once it sees them from a distance, they will hear it fuming and growling.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="13">
            وَإِذَآ أُلْقُوا۟ مِنْهَا مَكَانًۭا ضَيِّقًۭا مُّقَرَّنِينَ دَعَوْا۟ هُنَالِكَ ثُبُورًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="13">
            And when they are tossed into a narrow place inside ˹Hell˺, chained together, then and there they will cry out for ˹instant˺ destruction.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="14">
            لَّا تَدْعُوا۟ ٱلْيَوْمَ ثُبُورًۭا وَٰحِدًۭا وَٱدْعُوا۟ ثُبُورًۭا كَثِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="14">
            ˹They will be told,˺ “Do not cry only once for destruction, but cry many times over!”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="15">
            قُلْ أَذَٰلِكَ خَيْرٌ أَمْ جَنَّةُ ٱلْخُلْدِ ٱلَّتِى وُعِدَ ٱلْمُتَّقُونَ ۚ كَانَتْ لَهُمْ جَزَآءًۭ وَمَصِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="15">
            Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Is this better or the Garden of Eternity which the righteous have been promised, as a reward and ˹an ultimate˺ destination?
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="16">
            لَّهُمْ فِيهَا مَا يَشَآءُونَ خَٰلِدِينَ ۚ كَانَ عَلَىٰ رَبِّكَ وَعْدًۭا مَّسْـُٔولًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="16">
            There they will have whatever they wish for, forever. That is a promise ˹to be sought after˺, binding on your Lord.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="17">
            وَيَوْمَ يَحْشُرُهُمْ وَمَا يَعْبُدُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ فَيَقُولُ ءَأَنتُمْ أَضْلَلْتُمْ عِبَادِى هَٰٓؤُلَآءِ أَمْ هُمْ ضَلُّوا۟ ٱلسَّبِيلَ
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="17">
            ˹Watch for˺ the Day He will gather them along with what they used to worship besides Allah, and ask ˹the objects of worship˺, “Was it you who misled these servants of Mine, or did they stray from the Way ˹on their own˺?”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="18">
            قَالُوا۟ سُبْحَٰنَكَ مَا كَانَ يَنۢبَغِى لَنَآ أَن نَّتَّخِذَ مِن دُونِكَ مِنْ أَوْلِيَآءَ وَلَٰكِن مَّتَّعْتَهُمْ وَءَابَآءَهُمْ حَتَّىٰ نَسُوا۟ ٱلذِّكْرَ وَكَانُوا۟ قَوْمًۢا بُورًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="18">
            They will say, “Glory be to You! It was not right for ˹others like˺ us to take any lords besides You, but You allowed enjoyment for them and their forefathers ˹for so long˺ that they forgot ˹Your˺ remembrance and became a doomed people.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="19">
            فَقَدْ كَذَّبُوكُم بِمَا تَقُولُونَ فَمَا تَسْتَطِيعُونَ صَرْفًۭا وَلَا نَصْرًۭا ۚ وَمَن يَظْلِم مِّنكُمْ نُذِقْهُ عَذَابًۭا كَبِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="19">
            ˹The doomed will be told˺, “Your gods have clearly denied your claims. So now you can neither ward off ˹the punishment˺ nor get any help.” And whoever of you does wrong, We will make them taste a horrible punishment.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="20">
            وَمَآ أَرْسَلْنَا قَبْلَكَ مِنَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ إِلَّآ إِنَّهُمْ لَيَأْكُلُونَ ٱلطَّعَامَ وَيَمْشُونَ فِى ٱلْأَسْوَاقِ ۗ وَجَعَلْنَا بَعْضَكُمْ لِبَعْضٍۢ فِتْنَةً أَتَصْبِرُونَ ۗ وَكَانَ رَبُّكَ بَصِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="20">
            We never sent any messenger before you ˹O Prophet˺, who did not eat food and go about in market-places. We have made some of you a trial for others. Will you ˹not then˺ be patient? And your Lord is All-Seeing.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="21">
            ۞ وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يَرْجُونَ لِقَآءَنَا لَوْلَآ أُنزِلَ عَلَيْنَا ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ أَوْ نَرَىٰ رَبَّنَا ۗ لَقَدِ ٱسْتَكْبَرُوا۟ فِىٓ أَنفُسِهِمْ وَعَتَوْ عُتُوًّۭا كَبِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="21">
            Those who do not expect to meet Us say, “If only the angels were sent down to us, or we could see our Lord!” They have certainly been carried away by their arrogance and have entirely exceeded all limits.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="22">
            يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةَ لَا بُشْرَىٰ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لِّلْمُجْرِمِينَ وَيَقُولُونَ حِجْرًۭا مَّحْجُورًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="22">
            ˹But˺ on the Day they will see the angels, there will be no good news for the wicked, who will cry, “Keep away! Away ˹from us˺!”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="23">
            وَقَدِمْنَآ إِلَىٰ مَا عَمِلُوا۟ مِنْ عَمَلٍۢ فَجَعَلْنَٰهُ هَبَآءًۭ مَّنثُورًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="23">
            Then We will turn to whatever ˹good˺ deeds they did, reducing them to scattered dust.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="24">
            أَصْحَٰبُ ٱلْجَنَّةِ يَوْمَئِذٍ خَيْرٌۭ مُّسْتَقَرًّۭا وَأَحْسَنُ مَقِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="24">
            ˹But˺ on that Day the residents of Paradise will have the best settlement and the finest place to rest.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="25">
            وَيَوْمَ تَشَقَّقُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ بِٱلْغَمَٰمِ وَنُزِّلَ ٱلْمَلَٰٓئِكَةُ تَنزِيلًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="25">
            ˹Watch for˺ the Day the heavens will burst with clouds, and the angels will be sent down in successive ranks.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="26">
            ٱلْمُلْكُ يَوْمَئِذٍ ٱلْحَقُّ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ ۚ وَكَانَ يَوْمًا عَلَى ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ عَسِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="26">
            True authority on that Day will belong ˹only˺ to the Most Compassionate. And it will be a hard day for the disbelievers.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="27">
            وَيَوْمَ يَعَضُّ ٱلظَّالِمُ عَلَىٰ يَدَيْهِ يَقُولُ يَٰلَيْتَنِى ٱتَّخَذْتُ مَعَ ٱلرَّسُولِ سَبِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="27">
            And ˹beware of˺ the Day the wrongdoer will bite his nails ˹in regret˺ and say, “Oh! I wish I had followed the Way along with the Messenger!
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="28">
            يَٰوَيْلَتَىٰ لَيْتَنِى لَمْ أَتَّخِذْ فُلَانًا خَلِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="28">
            Woe to me! I wish I had never taken so-and-so as a close friend.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="29">
            لَّقَدْ أَضَلَّنِى عَنِ ٱلذِّكْرِ بَعْدَ إِذْ جَآءَنِى ۗ وَكَانَ ٱلشَّيْطَٰنُ لِلْإِنسَٰنِ خَذُولًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="29">
            It was he who truly made me stray from the Reminder after it had reached me.” And Satan has always betrayed humanity.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="30">
            وَقَالَ ٱلرَّسُولُ يَٰرَبِّ إِنَّ قَوْمِى ٱتَّخَذُوا۟ هَٰذَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ مَهْجُورًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="30">
            The Messenger has cried, “O my Lord! My people have indeed received this Quran with neglect.”
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="31">
            وَكَذَٰلِكَ جَعَلْنَا لِكُلِّ نَبِىٍّ عَدُوًّۭا مِّنَ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ ۗ وَكَفَىٰ بِرَبِّكَ هَادِيًۭا وَنَصِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="31">
            Similarly, We made enemies for every prophet from among the wicked, but sufficient is your Lord as a Guide and Helper.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="32">
            وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لَوْلَا نُزِّلَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلْقُرْءَانُ جُمْلَةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ لِنُثَبِّتَ بِهِۦ فُؤَادَكَ ۖ وَرَتَّلْنَٰهُ تَرْتِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="32">
            The disbelievers say, “If only the Quran had been sent down to him all at once!” ˹We have sent it˺ as such ˹in stages˺ so We may reassure your heart with it. And We have revealed it at a deliberate pace.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="33">
            وَلَا يَأْتُونَكَ بِمَثَلٍ إِلَّا جِئْنَٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَأَحْسَنَ تَفْسِيرًا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="33">
            Whenever they bring you an argument, We come to you with the right refutation and the best explanation.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="34">
            ٱلَّذِينَ يُحْشَرُونَ عَلَىٰ وُجُوهِهِمْ إِلَىٰ جَهَنَّمَ أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ شَرٌّۭ مَّكَانًۭا وَأَضَلُّ سَبِيلًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="34">
            Those who will be dragged into Hell on their faces will be in the worst place, and are ˹now˺ farthest from the ˹Right˺ Way.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="35">
            وَلَقَدْ ءَاتَيْنَا مُوسَى ٱلْكِتَٰبَ وَجَعَلْنَا مَعَهُۥٓ أَخَاهُ هَٰرُونَ وَزِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="35">
            We certainly gave Moses the Book and appointed his brother Aaron as his helper.
            </Aside>

        ---
        
            <Aside type="quran" title="36">
            فَقُلْنَا ٱذْهَبَآ إِلَى ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَٰتِنَا فَدَمَّرْنَٰهُمْ تَدْمِيرًۭا
            </Aside>

            <Aside type="reference" title="36">
            We had ordered ˹them˺, “Go to the people who would deny Our signs.” Then We annihilated the deniers entirely.
            </Aside>
