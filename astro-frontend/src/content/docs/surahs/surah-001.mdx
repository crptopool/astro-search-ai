---
title: Surah 1 - The Opener (Al-Fatihah)
description: Translation of Surah 1 - The Opener (Al-Fatihah)
---

**1.** (1) In the name of All?h,[2] the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful.[3]

**2.** (2) [All] praise is [due] to All?h, Lord[4] of the worlds -

**3.** (3) The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful,

**4.** (4) Sovereign of the Day of Recompense.[5]

**5.** (5) It is You we worship and You we ask for help.

**6.** (6) Guide us to the straight path -

**7.** (7) The path of those upon whom You have bestowed favor, not of those who have earned [Your] anger or of those who are astray.
