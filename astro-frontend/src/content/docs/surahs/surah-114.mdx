---
title: Surah 114 - The Mankind (An-Nas)
description: Translation of Surah 114 - The Mankind (An-Nas)
---

**1.** (1) Say, "I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind,

**2.** (2) The Sovereign of mankind,

**3.** (3) The God of mankind,

**4.** (4) From the evil of the retreating whisperer[2016] -

**5.** (5) Who whispers [evil] into the breasts of mankind -

**6.** (6) From among the jinn and mankind."[2017]
