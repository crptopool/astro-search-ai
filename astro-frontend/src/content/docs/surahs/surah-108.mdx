description: Translation of Surah 108 - The Abundance (Al-Kawthar)

**1.** (1) Indeed, We have granted you, [O Mu?ammad], al-Kawthar.

**2.** (2) So pray to your Lord and offer sacrifice [to Him alone].

**3.** (3) Indeed, your enemy is the one cut off.[2002]
