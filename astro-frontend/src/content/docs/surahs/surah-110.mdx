description: Translation of Surah 110 - The Divine Support (An-Nasr)
---

**1.** (1) When the victory of All?h has come and the conquest,[2005]

**2.** (2) And you see the people entering into the religion of All?h in multitudes,

**3.** (3) Then exalt [Him] with praise of your Lord and ask forgiveness of Him. Indeed, He is ever Accepting of Repentance.[2006]
