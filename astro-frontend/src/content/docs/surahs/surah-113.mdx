---
title: Surah 113 - The Daybreak (Al-Falaq)
description: Translation of Surah 113 - The Daybreak (Al-Falaq)
---

**1.** (1) Say, "I seek refuge in the Lord of daybreak

**2.** (2) From the evil of that which He created

**3.** (3) And from the evil of darkness when it settles

**4.** (4) And from the evil of the blowers in knots[2014]

**5.** (5) And from the evil of an envier when he envies."
