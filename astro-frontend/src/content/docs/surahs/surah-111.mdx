---
title: Surah 111 - The Palm Fiber (Al-Masad)
description: Translation of Surah 111 - The Palm Fiber (Al-Masad)
---

**1.** (1) May the hands of Ab? Lahab be ruined, and ruined is he.[2008]

**2.** (2) His wealth will not avail him or that which he gained.

**3.** (3) He will [enter to] burn in a Fire of [blazing] flame

**4.** (4) And his wife [as well] - the carrier of firewood.[2009]

**5.** (5) Around her neck is a rope of [twisted] fiber.
